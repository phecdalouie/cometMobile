Screen Repairs

Screen replacements can be expensive! To help our customers we offer a two tiered pricing system for our customers with screens that are only lightly damaged. We offer a broken glass price for screens without damaged LCD’s and just cracked glass. Also we have an LCD price for more damaged screens. LCD damage would be a screen that has gone black, has lines and or spots, yellowing, and no touch. All of our screens and parts carry a 60 day warranty. With both original and aftermarket screens available.
