Tempered Glass Screen Protectors

We offer tempered glass screen protectors for a variety of different devices. What is Tempered Glass? Tempered glass is a screen protector made of thin glass that is applied over your devices screen for extra protection. It acts as a first line of defense against drops by distributing the shock of the impact across the tempered glass first. The glass is extremely hard and it resists most scratches. You also do not have to worry as the tempered glass will not affect the function or touch sensitivity of your device. We offer tempered glass in a variety of different styles such as a privacy guard, bluelight filter, coloured, as well as plain glass.
